Secondary housing transaction information from the website of HomeLink Group (HLG).

I choose the data of the resold apartments because the new apartments are terribly deficient in Beijing and they are mostly located in suburb areas. Meanwhile, the market performance of new-property also represents the developer's ability to operating and marketing, which may overdraft future prices, while the price of the resold apartments is relatively real and objective. 

Web crawler based on Python

  • Convert the data format to .xlx document
  • Delete the data for villa residence, basement, and garage.
  • Delete the data with missing attributes.
  • Delete the data with obvious irrational attributes.
  • Filter the data according to specific property and do statistical analysis

The total amount of data crawled down is 17675 initially, and 15917 after data cleaning.

Every set of data has 15 properties -- description,  community,  floors, building time, window direction, layout, area, numbers of people interested in, numbers of people who have checked, published time, house price, the unit price per square meter, weblink, district, longitude, and latitude.
